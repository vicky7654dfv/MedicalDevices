Our Story

Two Decades of Building
Medical Excellence

From a single research lab in Boston to a global network of biomedical engineers, MediLife has grown by solving the problems that matter most to patients and clinicians alike.

Our Origin & Growth

MediLife was founded in 2003 by two biomedical engineers who believed that hospital equipment failures were not inevitable — they were preventable. Starting with a five-person team in a compact Boston workshop, we began servicing diagnostic imaging equipment for local clinics.

As our reputation for reliability grew, so did our mandate. By 2009 we had opened our first international office in London, followed swiftly by expansions into Singapore, Dubai, and São Paulo.
